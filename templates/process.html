Our Soap Making Process

Traditional methods meeting modern standards

01

Ingredient Selection

We carefully select premium natural ingredients from trusted local suppliers. Our ingredients include:

  • Pure Shea Butter from Northern Ghana
  • Organic Coconut Oil
  • Essential Oils
  • Natural Colorants
02

Hand Mixing

Each batch is mixed with precision and care using traditional methods:

  • Temperature-controlled mixing
  • Small batch production
  • Careful monitoring of saponification
03

Curing Process

Our soaps are cured for 4-6 weeks to ensure:

  • Proper hardening
  • Gentle pH levels
  • Long-lasting quality
04

Quality Control

Every bar undergoes rigorous quality checks:

  • pH testing
  • Visual inspection
  • Weight consistency
  • Fragrance evaluation
