About LaVida Soaps

Our story of natural skincare

Our Story

LaVida Soap Company began in 2020 with a simple mission: to create pure, natural soaps that nourish your skin and respect the environment. Located in the heart of Accra, we've grown from a small home-based operation to a trusted name in natural skincare.

Our Values

  • Natural Ingredients
  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Traditional Methods
  • Local Sourcing

Our Commitment

We source our ingredients from local suppliers, supporting Ghanaian farmers and ensuring the highest quality materials for our soaps. Each bar is handcrafted with care, maintaining traditional soap-making methods while incorporating modern sustainability practices.

Community Impact

LaVida Soap Company is proud to be part of the local community. We regularly conduct workshops to teach soap-making skills and provide employment opportunities to local artisans.
